The Boy and the Devil Suit follows Luca, an ordinary young man whose life changes after a chance encounter on a train. A wounded fugitive named Desem—pursued by the Justice Federal Force (JF Force)—is carrying a mysterious briefcase containing the Devil Suit, a powerful and dangerous artifact. Before dying, Desem performs a blood ritual that binds Luca to the suit, making him its new host. Soon after, Luca meets Hendrix, who was meant to retrieve the Devil Suit from Desem. Hendrix reveals that Luca is now wanted by the JF Force, forcing them both to go on the run as Luca struggles to understand the power—and danger—he now carries
